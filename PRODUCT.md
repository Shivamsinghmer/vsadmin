# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Admin operators at VS Enterprises — a B2B industrial product business (fasteners, bolts, steel rods, hardware components). Users are the business owner or operations staff, working on a laptop or desktop, managing day-to-day fulfilment and content updates.

## Product Purpose

A private admin panel for VS Enterprises to manage their B2B e-commerce operations: process and track orders (status updates, invoice upload, GST tracking), manage a product catalogue with variable pricing matrices (diameter × length × material grade), and manage categories, blog posts, and customer testimonials.

## Brand Personality

Industrial, precise, friendly. The business is rigorous and technical, but the tool should feel approachable — operators should feel in control without feeling overwhelmed.

## Anti-references

- Pitch-black "hacker" dashboards with neon accents
- Slow, over-animated SaaS tools with choreographed page-load sequences
- Flat monochrome dashboards with no visual hierarchy
- Overly playful consumer app aesthetics that don't match an industrial B2B context

## Design Principles

1. **Density serves precision**: Information-dense tables with clear hierarchy over decorative whitespace
2. **State is always visible**: Every piece of changeable data shows its state clearly
3. **Consistent vocabulary**: Same buttons, table patterns, and form controls on every page
4. **Friendly precision**: Industrial accuracy without intimidating opacity
5. **Content leads, chrome follows**: Navigation and shell disappear into the background; the data is the product

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast. All interactive elements keyboard-navigable. No motion-only state indicators.
